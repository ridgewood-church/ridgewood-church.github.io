The Gospel

The heart of the gospel is redemption, and the essence of redemption is the substitutionary sacrifice.~ C.H. Spurgeon

The gospel is the singularly most important communication of God to man. In Jesus, who is God the Son, we have the revelation of God's love and sacrifice that saves us from God's righteous judgement upon sinners.

If you are not a Christian and want to know how to be forgiven of your sins and follow Christ or are just curious to know what the Christian gospel is, then we invite you to visit a multimedia presentation of the gospel by Matthias Media entitled Two Ways to Live

If you are struggling with life's many questions and want to know how the gospel is pertinent to every area of life, then we would like to point you to some videos by Truelife.org that answer life's hard questions
