About Us

As an Acts 1:8 church, we at Ridgewood are committed to being fully Gospel-minded in every area of our lives.

We are real people, who "do life" together, and we orient our lives toward declaring the Gospel, by learning together how to effectively reach out to our community, state, nation and world.

Feel free to join us this Sunday, or click on one of the links to the right to learn more about us.
