Education

Since Ridgewood believes that education is inseparable from discipleship, it is natural for us to offer help and support families in this area whether homeschooling, private schooling, or government schooling.
  1. Homeschool Help

    From families who have been at it for years, to those considering home education for the first time, Ridgewood Church is here to support you. No longer do home educators have to find a support group outside the 'walls' of the church. Ridgewood Church offers encouragement, resources, and inspiration to homeschooling families.

    Anyone interested in homeschooling can contact us at office@ridgewoodchurch.us to receive encouragement and guidance.

    Learn more about homeschooling by visiting http://www.consideringhomeschooling.org and http://www.hslda.org

  2. Private School Help

    Since 1998, Ridgewood Christian School has been working to provide a quality education to hundreds of young people while building their character. The school has continually taught high academics and traditional values to students in pre-kindergarten through 12th grade. The secondary school (7-12) is Southeast Texas' first certified University Model School.  In 2014, RCS became Lighthouse Christian Academy.  LCA still meets on the campus of Ridgewood Church, but has become its own non-profit organization in order to widen the impact of Kingdom education in the SETX area.

    The student body at Lighthouse Christian School resides in more than 11 local cities and attends more than 40 local churches of different denominations. Graduates from Ridgewood Christian School (now LCA) have been accepted to Lamar University, University of Texas, East Texas Baptist University, Houston Baptist University, West Coast Bible College, Lamar Institute of Technology, Abilene Christian College and more.  Lighthouse gives parents of the Golden Triangle a choice in the education of their children. LCA gives children a chance to be educated with high morals and values.

    For more information click on the new website http://www.lighthousesetx.org 

    Learn more about the University Model by visiting http://www.naums.net
